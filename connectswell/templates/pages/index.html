Key Features

Stress Reduction

We leverage a network of local professional wellness practitioners to provide services when they are needed most- directly in the office or adjacent facilities.

Our flexible scheduling is designed with busy wo-working spaces in mind. Create a workplace culture that encourages wellness and reduces stress in your community through easy-to-implement classes.
Stress has now become the number one cause of workplace absenteeism according to new research. Save time and money by bringing wellness directly into your office, and reap the benefits of a healthier workplace.
Students are increasingly susceptible to stress, burnout and anxiety, especially around busy times of the year with stress management techniques becoming increasingly important for mental health.
Posture

Blossome equips your workspace with the supplies and know-how to run effective on-site physical wellness classes.

Ergonomics is the science behind the design of the working environment. We teach you how to optimize working spaces for human use and efficiency with the result of increased productivity and reduced strain.
Yoga postures, or asana, are useful in developing proper posture. We provide all necessary supplies for classes and our all of our movement classes are taught by certified and insured professional teachers.
Massage, Reiki and FST brought directly in the office form the basis of our in office stress reduction approach, allowing individuals to relax and de-stress.
Attention and Focus

We use high tech biosensing tools to help our clients understand subjective and objective wellness markers.

Our innovative Biosensing tools are sensors that detect heart rate, breath and Electroencephalogram (EEg)- also known as brain scanning.
These tools allow us peek into the hidden functioning inside the body, to track wellness markers and determine effectiveness of wellness programs through a data driven approach.
Neurofeedback is the process of "mirroring" the central nervous system, or CNS back on itself using an EEg brain scan. This self reflective technique has been shown to have many benefits in stress reduction and focus.

Our innovative programs are changing the role of wellness in organizations of all types and sizes.
