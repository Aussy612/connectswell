Key Features

Stress Reduction

We leverage a network of local professional wellness pracitioners to provide services when they are needed most- directly in the office or adjacent facilites.

Our flexible scheduling is designed with busy wo-working spaces in mind. Create a workplace culture that encourages wellness and reduces stress in your community through easy-to-implement classes.
Stress has now become the number one cause of workplace absenteeism according to new research. Save time and money by bringing wellness directly into your office, and reap the benefits of a healthier workplace.
Students are increasingly susceptible to stress, burnout and anxiety, especially around busy times of the year with stress management techniques becoming increasingly important for mental health.
Posture

Blossome equips your workspace with the supplies and know-how to run effective on-site physical wellness classes.

Ergonomics is the science behind the design of the working environment. We teach you how to optimize working spaces for ideal human use and efficiency with the result of increased productivity and reduced strain.
Yoga postures, or asana, are useful in developing proper posture. We provide all necessary supplies for classes and our yoga and movement classes are taught by certified and insured professional teachers.
Massage, Reiki and FST brought directly in the office form the basis of our in office stress reduction approach.
