Practitioners

Blossome provides inspiring wellness practitioners to more opportunities to work.

Apply, lead classes, get paid

We match your services with organizations that need them. Our platform streamlines marketing and scheduling of corporate wellness so you can focus on what you do best: delivering transformative wellness content.

Complete the application process and in-person or phone interview. If it seems like a good fit, you will be invited to attend one of our upcoming meeting days to get comfortable with our system and learn techniques for bringing wellness to a workspace.

A core element of our system is that every program is unique- there's no set script here. We encourage practitioners to teach what they are passionate about!

Generally, our service offerings include courses in our three main categories below.

Our programs are flexible to allow clients to try a variety of practices to see what works best for them. We work with a network of independent practitioners to provide on-demand services directly in organizations. You can work with other practitioners and provide a unique services at different times. You get paid immediately once your classes are completed!
